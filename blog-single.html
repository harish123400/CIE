AVISHKAR: A deep tech acceleration program

  • 3rd May 2016
  • Saarang
  • General, Media

Scientists, across the world, believe in coupling cognitive abilities with machine learning algorithms to unleash the limitless potential of Artificial Intelligence [AI]. Over the last few decades, machine learning has quietly and slowly crept into being an integral part of our lives. From spell checking dictionaries on your text editor to query search rankings, web page indexing and classification to Facebook friend’s suggestions based on user habits and details. These being just a few examples of machine learning algorithms amongst countless others. The onus has been on improving human comfort and pursuing mankind’s thirst for knowledge over the last few centuries. It extremely evident, moving forward, Artificial Intelligence and machine learning have a bigger role to play. The products and solutions offered in our immediate foreseeable future are going to be self-learning by design. Therefore, revolutionizing both the way we live and work and establishing new benchmarks for the technological world, helping push the envelope further.

Similarly, we are also on the verge of a cultural revolution. Augmented and Virtual reality devices have been around for the last 3-4 decades, and have seen extensive use in specialized fields like the military and health care industry. Their prolonged run as a niche product was mostly due to the limitations of technologies available during this time. These products never crossed paths with the average consumer mostly due to their exorbitant prices. But recent technological developments have changed that. Introduction of cost-effective AR and VR headgear in the shape of wearable helmets has opened up a new dimension in the world of entertainment. They offer a viewing experience with unparalleled immersion by putting the user in the middle of the action instead of him being an observer from a distance. With every major player in the world looking to grab a share of the market, AR/VR technology is the fastest growing market since the smartphone revolution. Our viewing experiences and cultural growth is going to be catalyzed with the rise of this technology. Who knows, the acceptance and widespread use of AR/VR in the future might even be labeled as a period of modern day renaissance.

This paradigm shift, has been recognized by the IIIT-H Foundation. To be a part of this movement we have launched a program titled ‘AVISHKAR’, an accelerator program singularly focused in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, machine learning and Virtual and Augmented Reality. A first in India. This program, launched in partnership with 50k Ventures, T-Hub and CoCreation Consulting, aims to bridge the gap startups face when they set out to achieve their goals in these uncharted territories. By providing the right kind of aid and assistance, we hope to develop the products and solutions for the world of tomorrow.

AVISHKAR
A gist:
As already mentioned above, it is an Accelerator program, launched by IIIT-H foundation launched in partnership with 50k Ventures, T-Hub and CoCreation Consulting. A first of its kind, this program intends to cater to startups who’ve ventured into the fields of Artificial Intelligence, image and speech technologies, language processing technologies, other machine learning fields and Augmented and Virtual Reality.

IIIT-H Foundation is an integral part of the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. Even though we are based out of Hyderabad, the program is open to everybody across the country. Though we would prefer startups to relocate to Hyderabad, in case an organization is unable to do so, they can still apply as long as the founders spend two weeks with us every quarter.

Program Structure:
This program is a structured 6-month long program. Upto 10 startups will be selected by the Investment Committee over the course of a year. Once a startup is selected, it will be funded in the form of convertible notes. We are fully aware that every startup is unique and different from the rest. Hence, we are going to create an extremely flexible and moldable custom made program for every individual startup to allow them to flourish. The progress of startups will be reviewed along with the Investment Committee every 3 months. Each cohort will finally graduate with a demo day, a presentation in front of various investors, VC’s and senior representatives from the industry.

Resources Offered:
The resources we bring to the table are going to form the foundation for the startups, giving them a platform to achieve success. IIIT Hyderabad, with its reputation of being one of the best research universities in the country, provides access to IIIT Hyderabad research labs and expertise in these fields to help startups create technologically sound products. Startups will gain access to and work with the large business mentor network of T-Hub. 50k Ventures will guide them through their following round of investments during and after the program. CoCreation Consulting along with IIIT-H Foundation have designed and will run the program and essentially coach the selected startups as they move forward.


Timeline:
Application deadline for Cohort I April 30th
Start of Batch 1 May 15th
Graduation Day November 14th
Start of Batch 2 October 15th

For further information and assistance, please visit our website. Alternatively, you can get get in touch with us directly at contact@iiith.org
